Rules:

Ping-pong will count all your numbers from 1 with the following exceptions:

  • Every number divisible by 3 shall be replaced with "ping"
  • Every number divisible by 5 shall be replaced with "pong"
  • Every number divisible by 15 shall be replaced with "pingpong"
